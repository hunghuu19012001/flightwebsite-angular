What to expect

Make the most out of your visit to Vietnam and skip the long lines at the immigration area of the airport with this Standard Fast-Track Service. Giving you access to the Priority Immigration Lane, you'll be able to reduce the boredom of waiting, as well as the large crowds! Simply book the service on the 2VNTravel website/app, indicate your flight number and time arrival and get instant confirmation! Once you're at the airport, you'll be greeted by a friendly staff member holding a sign with your name on it, who will guide you through the formalities at the airport, including passing through customs with the convenient Priority Immigration Lane, guaranteeing a hassle-free experience at the airport.
